Magicalll Frostval Cloak
This item required a Dragon Amulet. It is a mystical wintery cloak that trails snow behind you, in any temperature. Happy Frostval!

Location: Gift Boxes
Price: 1,000 Gold
Sellback: 100 Gold

Level: 60
Element: Ice
Stats: Crit +5, Magic Def +4, Pierce Def +4, Melee Def +4, WIS +5, END +5, CHA +5, INT +2, DEX +2, STR +2, Bonus +5
Resists: Ice +10, Water +4

Rarity: 0
Item Type: Cape
Equip Spot: Back
Category: Armor

Other information
  • If you wear it long enough, a snow cloud will form over your head, and it'll snow over you. While this happens, it plays the little hero Frostvale tune.
