Other information
  • Items cannot be destroyed.
  • Wings help provide a series of special effects when used with any Astravian Wingblade, Astravian Wingstaff, or Astravian Wingknife visibly equipped; additional special effects occur with any Astravian Helm equipped.
  • Wings are animated; when the player begins moving the wings dip down, raising again once the player resumes an idle position.
  • Wings were tiered with Roman numerals and became unsellable on September 22nd, 2023 and September 29th, 2023.
