Other information
  • Helms help provide a +75% damage boost against targets of 'Dragon' race when used with any DragonKnight Cape equipped and any DragonKnight Blade / Staff / Dagger slotted, shown, or visibly equipped.
  • Special effect is applied multiplicatively with any other damage multipliers.
  • Click on the visor to change between two appearances.
  • Helms were tiered with Roman numerals on February 8th, 2025.
