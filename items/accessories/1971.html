Pactogonal Knight Helm
This is the style of helm worn by the Knights of the Pactogonal Table. It adds to your Bonus, slightly increasing your chance to hit.
(No DA Required)

Location: Sir Vey's Shop
Price: N/A
Required Item: 1 Pouch of Sparkling Sand
Sellback: 20 Gold

Level: 1
Element: Metal
Stats: INT +1, DEX +1, STR +1, Bonus +4
Resists: Darkness +2, Light +2

Rarity: 3
Item Type: Helm
Equip Spot: Head
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Helm previously required 5 Pouch of Sparkling Sand; this was lowered to 1 on March 21st, 2025.
  • The following changes were implemented on March 28th, 2025:
    • Previously had the description: This is the style of helm worn by the Knights of the Pactogonal Table. It adds to your Str, Int and Dex.
    • Bonus +4 stat was added.
