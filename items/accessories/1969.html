Pactogonal Knight Belt
This sturdy belt is the same kind worn by the Knights of the Pactogonal Table. It increases your Block, Parry, and Dodge Defenses, making it slightly more likely for you to Defend against attacks.
(No DA Required)

Location: Sir Vey's Shop
Price: N/A
Required Item: 1 Pouch of Sparkling Sand
Sellback: 20 Gold

Level: 1
Element: Metal
Stats: Block +4, Dodge +4, Parry +4, Crit +1
Resists: None

Rarity: 3
Item Type: Belt
Equip Spot: Waist
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Belt previously required 5 Pouch of Sparkling Sand; this was lowered to 1 on March 21st, 2025.

    • Block stat was updated from +1 to +4.
    • Parry and Dodge stats were added.
