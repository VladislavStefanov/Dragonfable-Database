Other information
  • Items cannot be destroyed.
  • Helms are color-custom; the hat, the trim, the ribbon, and the stitching are linked to the player's base, trim, dragon eye, and dragon wing colors respectively.
  • Helms overlay the player's chosen hairstyle; these helms were the first to have this functionality, as indicated in the October 1st, 2021 Design Notes.
  • Helms were temporarily available in the October DC Specials shop during their annual month of availability between October 1st, 2021 and November 3rd, 2023.
