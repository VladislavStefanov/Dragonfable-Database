Sellback: 350 Gold

Level: 20

Stats: Block +5, Dodge +5, Parry +5, WIS +5, INT +2, DEX +2, STR +2
Resists: Poison +10
Ability: Summon Kunai (1)

Rarity: 25
Item Type: Trinket
Equip Spot: Trinket
Category: Armor

Other information
  • The following changes were implemented on September 1st, 2023:
    • Previously had the description: The ancient scrolls had a passage on how to summon an illusionary version of the mighty Honda. The illusion is so strong that it will attack and poison your foe! This item requires a Dragon Amulet.
    • Trinket's name was changed from 'Summon Honda' to 'Honda's Legacy'.
    • Trinket's Resist was lowered from +25 Poison to +10 Poison.
    • Trinket's ability was replaced from Summon Honda to Summon Kunai (1).
