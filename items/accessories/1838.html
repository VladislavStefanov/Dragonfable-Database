Other information
  • Dragon Coin versions were only available during Talk Like a Pirate Day 2018; permanent low drop rate versions are available from any random encounter quest in The Pale Sea or The Pasgur Ocean (excluding Treasure!).
  • Items occupy the player's helm slot when equipped.
  • Artifact helms are color-custom; the hair, the hat, the trim and emblem, and the feathers and bandana are linked to the player's hair, base, trim, and dragon skin colors respectively.
  • Artifact helms' color-custom hair was added on October 26th, 2018, while all components were made color-custom on September 24th, 2021.
  • Previously equipping any Navigator's Hat artifact helm was required to modify the appearance of the Pirate class; they were updated to unlock Navigator Armor Customization options on September 17th, 2021.
