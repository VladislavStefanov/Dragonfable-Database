Suede Frostval Top Hat
This item requires a Dragon Amulet. You got this hat from a gift box given to you the moglins of Frostvale. Once equipped, click on hat for wintery surprise!

Location: Moglin Gift
Price: 1,000 Gold
Sellback: 100 Gold

Level: 30
Element: Ice
Bonuses: Block +1, Crit +3, Magic Def +2, Pierce Def +2, Melee Def +2, END +5, INT +2, STR +2, Bonus +4
Resists: Ice +5, Darkness +5

Rarity: 1
Item Type: Helm
Equip Spot: Head
Category: Armor

Other information
  • When you click on the helm, a cloud appears and it snows while the Frostval music plays.
