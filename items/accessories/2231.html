Other information
  • Helm is automatically upgraded to the highest level a player can use; once a new level threshold has been reached, players must relog to obtain the new version.
  • Helms are animated; the headphones glow, the ear pads pulse, and the antenna emits a blue sonar wave periodically.
  • Helms are color-custom; the hair is linked to the player's hair color.
  • Helms were DC-tagged on December 30th, 2020.
