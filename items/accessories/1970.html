Pactogonal Knight Cape
This stylish cape is the color and style favored by the Knights of the Pactogonal Table. It increases your Melee, Pierce, and Magic Avoidance, making it slightly more likely for you to Avoid attacks.
(No DA Required)

Location: Sir Vey's Shop
Price: N/A
Required Item: 1 Pouch of Sparkling Sand
Sellback: 2 Gold

Level: 5
Element: None
Stats: Magic Def +4, Pierce Def +4, Melee Def +4
Resists: None

Rarity: 2
Item Type: Cape
Equip Spot: Back
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Cape previously required 5 Pouch of Sparkling Sand; this was lowered to 1 on March 21st, 2025.
  • The following changes were implemented on March 28th, 2025:
    • The description's excerpt It increases your Melee, Pierce, and Magic Avoidance, making it slightly more likely for you to Avoid attacks. was added.
    • Melee Def was updated from +1 to +4.
    • Pierce Def and Magic Def stats were added.
