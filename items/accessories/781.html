Dragon's Rage
This small purple sphere shudders with power. It resonates with Dragon Amulets to increase the offensive power of DragonLords at the cost of defensive ability. This item requires a Dragon Amulet to use. (DCs)

Location: DragonLord Artifacts
Price: 250 Dragon Coins
Sellback: 225 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 63 Dragon Coins after 24 hours

Level: 25
Element: Good
Stats: Crit +4, Magic Def +2, Pierce Def +2, Melee Def +2, WIS +2, END +2, INT +6, DEX +6, STR +6, Bonus +5
Resists: Light +5, Darkness +5
Ability: DragonLord's Rage
Modifies: DragonLord

Combine 1 with Dragoncore Stone to form Dragon's Wrath
Combine 1 with Dragoncore Crystal to form Dragon's Wrath

Rarity: 100
Item Type: Artifact
Equip Spot: Trinket
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Item occupies the player's trinket slot when equipped.
  • Previously only required players to be Level 1 to equip this artifact trinket; this was changed to Level 25 on June 25th, 2021.
  • The prices of both Gold and DC versions of this item were originally priced at 325,000 Gold and 750 Dragon Coins, with a sellback of 32,500 Gold and 675 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 188 Dragon Coins after 24 hours respectively; this was changed to 25,000 Gold and 250 Dragon Coins, with the remaining 300,000 Gold and 500 Dragon Coins transposed to Dragoncore Stone and Dragoncore Crystal respectively, on August 6th, 2021.
  • Equipping this artifact trinket automatically changes the appearance of any Visage of the Dragon helm equipped or shown.
  • Both Gold and Dragon Coins descriptions of this item were updated to differentiate both versions on September 6th, 2024.
  • A trinket skill was added on June 20th, 2025.
