Golden Helm of Nobility
This is one of the helms worn by the ancient order of Palis Duetorum, a sect of Paladins sworn with the protection and healing of the innocent. It allows the one who wears it an additional 3% healing from EVERY healing effect.
(No DA Required)

Location: Transformed from Noble Chest (Normal)
Price: N/A

Element: None
Bonuses: Parry +1, Melee Def +1, END +2, CHA +2, DEX +2
Resists: Light +1, Health -3

Rarity: 7
Item Type: Helm
