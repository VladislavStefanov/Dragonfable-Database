Magicalllll Frostval Cloak

Sellback: 100 Gold

Element: Ice
Stats: Crit +7, Magic Def +6, Pierce Def +6, Melee Def +6, WIS +10, END +10, CHA +10, INT +6, DEX +6, STR +6, Bonus +7
Resists: Ice +14, Water +8, Health -2

Rarity: 0
Item Type: Cape
Equip Spot: Back
Category: Armor

Other information
  • If you wear it long enough, a snow cloud will form over your head, and it'll snow over you. While this happens, it plays the little hero Frostvale tune.
