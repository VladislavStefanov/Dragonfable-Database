Mystical Frostval Cloak
This item required a Dragon Amulet. It is a magical wintery cloak that trails snow behind you, in any temperature. Happy Frostval!

Location: Frostval 2010, Gift Boxes
Price: 1,000 Gold
Sellback: 100 Gold

Level: 40
Element: Ice
Stats: Crit +3, Magic Def +2, Pierce Def +2, Melee Def +2, END +4, CHA +4, Bonus +2
Resists: Ice +6

Rarity: 0
Item Type: Cape
Equip Spot: Back
Category: Armor

Other information
  • If you wear it long enough, a snow cloud will form over your head, and it'll snow over you. While this happens, it plays the little hero Frostvale tune.
