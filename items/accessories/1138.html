Frostval Top Hat
This item requires a Dragon Amulet. You got this hat from a gift box given to you the moglins of Frostvale. Once equipped, click on hat for wintery surprise!

Location: Moglin Gift
Price: 1,000 Gold
Sellback: 100 Gold

Level: 6
Element: Ice
Stats: Block +1, Crit +2, Magic Def +1, Pierce Def +1, Melee Def +1, END +3, INT +1, STR +1, Bonus +4
Resists: Ice +3

Rarity: 1
Item Type: Helm
Equip Spot: Head
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Transformed from Green Present on December 31st, 2007.
  • When you click on the helm, a cloud appears and it snows while the Frostval music plays.
