This small blue sphere spins with power. When close to a Dragon Amulet, the sphere glows brighter. This trinket raises the power of a defensive DragonLord. This item requires a Dragon Amulet to use.

Location: DragonLord Artifacts
Price: N/A
Required Items: 1 Dragon's Patience (D-Amulet/D-Coins) & 1 Dragoncore Crystal OR 1 Dragon's Patience (D-Amulet/D-Coins) & 1 Dragoncore Stone
Sellback: 0 Dragon Coins

Level: 55
Element: Good
Stats: Block +5, Dodge +5, Parry +5, Crit +5, Magic Def +5, Pierce Def +5, Melee Def +5, WIS +15, END +15, INT +6, DEX +6, STR +6, Bonus +5
Resists: Light +5, Darkness +5, All +3, Health -3
Ability: DragonLord's Patience
Modifies: DragonLord

Rarity: 100
Item Type: Artifact
Equip Spot: Trinket
Category: Armor

Other information
  • Item occupies the player's trinket slot when equipped.
  • Gold and DC versions of this item initially had the sellback values of 32,500 Gold and 188 Dragon Coins respectively.
  • The Dragoncore Stone only upgraded the Gold version and the Dragoncore Crystal only upgraded the DC version between August 6th, 2021 and September 6th, 2024, when merge compatibility for all versions was added.
  • Equipping this artifact trinket automatically changes the appearance of any Visage of the Dragon helm equipped or shown.
  • A trinket skill was added on June 20th, 2025.
