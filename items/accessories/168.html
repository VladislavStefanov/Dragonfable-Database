    Other information
  • Items occupy the player's helm slot when equipped.
  • Artifact helms are animated; the soul piece crackles with energy periodically in its Energy elemental attunement appearance, and part of the soul piece spirals continuously in its Poison elemental attunement appearance.
  • Artifact helms are color-custom; the aventail is linked to the player's base color.
  • Artifact helms' soul piece is attuned to the player's equipped weapon's element; refer to this chart for all appearances.
  • During April Fools' Day 2018, the level 60 and 80 versions modified the Fleshweaver class.
  • Level 60 and 80 versions' names were differentiated with stat indicators and tiered with Roman numerals on July 1st, 2023.
  • The following changes were implemented on August 23rd, 2024:
    • Level 60 and 80 versions previously had the description: This helm once belonged to Baltael, the first SoulWeaver. The power it bestows shows you how to fully Synchronize with Aegis, enhancing your Soul Aegis's defensive power, Valour Impact's number of hits, and SoulSync's damage.
    • Level 60 and 80 versions' Resists were updated.
    • Skilled and Master versions were released, balancing the benefits of their precursors, but requiring a Dragon Amulet to equip.
  • Synched version was released as a direct upgrade to the Skilled and Master versions, also requiring a Dragon Amulet to equip, on September 6th, 2024.
