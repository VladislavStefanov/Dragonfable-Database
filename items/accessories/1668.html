Magicallll Frostval Cloak
This item required a Dragon Amulet. It is a mystical wintery cloak that trails snow behind you, in any temperature. Happy Frostval!

Location: Gift Boxes
Price: 1,000 Gold
Sellback: 100 Gold

Level: 70
Element: Ice
Stats: Crit +6, Magic Def +5, Pierce Def +5, Melee Def +5, WIS +7, END +7, CHA +7, INT +4, DEX +4, STR +4, Bonus +6
Resists: Ice +12, Water +6

Rarity: 0
Item Type: Cape
Equip Spot: Back
Category: Armor

Other information
  • If you wear it long enough, a snow cloud will form over your head, and it'll snow over you. While this happens, it plays the little hero Frostvale tune.
