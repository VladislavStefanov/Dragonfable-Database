  • Any version of these helms must be equipped or shown to begin the following quests: Roots of Change, The Way Forward, Through the Tangle, The Deep Dark, Into the Abyss, Fate's Flow (Part 1), and Fate's Flow (Part 2).
  • Helms are animated; blue-to-pink gradient rotates within the helm continuously.
  • Helms overlay the player's chosen hairstyle.
