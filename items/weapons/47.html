Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Upon landing the first hit, permanently apply 'Exp Up' to player. While affected by 'Exp Up', increases Exp gains by +10%. (Rate: 100%)




Other information
  • Weapon's special can only be activated once per battle and requires a Dragon Amulet to activate.
  • Weapon's special is affected by X-Boost modification, effectively yielding a maximum of +110% Exp rewarded per battle with 10 stacks of X-Boost: Unlimited.
  • Weapon previously required Level 10 to equip; this was lowered to Level 9 on March 21st, 2025, before getting lowered even further to Level 8 on September 5th, 2025.
