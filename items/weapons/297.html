Big 100K Scythe
This is the counterpart to the Mighty 100k, the most expensive gold weapon in DragonFable! Cysero used his connections to get a copy of it. If you can't see yourself farming 100,000 gold for a weapon, this is a great alternative!
(No DA Required)

Location: Cysero's Weapons
Price: 400 Dragon Coins
Sellback: 360 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 100 Dragon Coins after 24 hours

Level: 50
Damage: 65-77
Element: Silver
Bonuses: Crit +4, END +5, INT +4, DEX +4, STR +4, Bonus +2
Resists: Light +5, Darkness +5, Water +5, Fire +5

Rarity: 10
Item Type: Scythe
Damage Type: Magic/Pierce/Melee
Equip Spot: Weapon
Category: Weapon

  • Weapon is animated; the loops at the top of the scythe spin continuously.
