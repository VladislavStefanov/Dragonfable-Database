Other information
  • Click anywhere on the weapon (excluding the lower hilt) to change its appearance between normal (default) and the eight main elemental appearances; element is locked to Good and does not change with each elemental appearance.
  • Weapons are animated; orbs of elemental energy appear between elemental appearance transitions, and the blade glints when transitioning back into its default state.
  • Light appearance's blade is translucent.
