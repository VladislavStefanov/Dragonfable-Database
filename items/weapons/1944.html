The Grim Ace
This large sword is amazingly light and well balanced, but requires a Dragon Amulet to wield. A special process was used to prepare the magic blade adding a purple tint to it.

Location: Cysero's Weapons
Price: 130 Dragon Coins
Sellback: 117 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 33 Dragon Coins after 24 hours

Level: 3
Damage: 17-21
Element: Metal
Bonuses: Crit +1, STR +1, Bonus +2
Resists: None

Rarity: 4
Item Type: Sword
Damage Type: Melee
Equip Spot: Weapon
Category: Weapon

Other information
  • Weapon is a reference to the McDonaldland character Grimace.
  • Weapon was initially available to purchase at Cysero's Shop until the release of Cysero's Superstore of Savings! on September 21st, 2007.
