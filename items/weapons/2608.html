Other information
  • While this weapon's item type is Sword, its damage type is erroneously Magic instead of Melee.
  • Weapon's inventory icon is erroneously a Scythe, despite being a Sword.
  • Weapon is animated; the blade vibrates rapidly, while spikes appear and disappear from the base of the blade cyclically, and the Blade of Destiny piece glows continuously.
  • Weapon's special activation was updated from On-Attack to On-Hit in February 2021.
  • Weapon's special activation rate was lowered from 30% to 5% on April 3rd, 2021.
