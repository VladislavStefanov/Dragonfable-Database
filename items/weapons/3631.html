Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Applies 'Unhallowed Leech' (+5% Boost) to target for 5 turns inclusive. (Pop-up: You leech some power from your foe!) (Rate: 10%)


Appearance


Other information
  • Weapons cannot be destroyed.
  • Weapons are animated; the red flames flicker, the orange shards glow, the hilt crackles with energy, and the red eye glows sporadically.
  • Weapons' special was buffed from 1% chance for +5% Boost to player for 2 turns inclusive on October 18th, 2019.
