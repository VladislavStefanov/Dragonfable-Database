Other information
  • Weapons have a low drop rate from the quest The Show Mustn't Go On!.

    • Prior to unlocking an Armor Customization option on November 27th, 2020, equipping any of these weapons modifies the appearance of the DoomKnight class by changing its shoulder plates.
