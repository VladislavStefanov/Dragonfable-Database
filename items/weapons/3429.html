Special
Activate your weapon's special!

Effect: Purges all status effects (excluding 'Stuffed', 'Doomed' from DoomKnight / DoomKnight V1, and permanent effects) from player; consumes [20X] MP from player; applies 'Purified Heal', a [Level] HoT effect, to player for [5-X] turns inclusive.

Cooldown: 16 - [(Player LUK/50), rounded down]
Charge Time: 8 - [(Player LUK/100), rounded down]

Other information
  • This On-Demand special is only exclusive to Revenant's Remnant.
  • X is equal to the total number of status effects purged.
    • The MP consumption will cap at 100.
    • If X is greater than or equal to 5, then 'Purified Heal' will not be applied.
  • If the player has less than 100 MP upon using the special, it will consume all remaining MP.
