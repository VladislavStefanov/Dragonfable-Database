Robble
This item requires a Dragon Amulet. Its dual blades strike at your foes with a hunger that only a true burgler could possess.

Location: Cysero's Weapons
Price: 130 Dragon Coins
Sellback: 117 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 33 Dragon Coins after 24 hours

Level: 3
Damage: 17-21
Element: Metal
Bonuses: Crit +1, DEX +1
Resists: None

Rarity: 4
Item Type: Dagger
Damage Type: Pierce
Equip Spot: Weapon
Category: Weapon

Other information
  • Weapon is a reference to the McDonaldland character Hamburgler's catchphrase, "Robble Robble".
  • Weapon was initially available to purchase at Cysero's Shop until the release of Cysero's Superstore of Savings! on September 21st, 2007.
