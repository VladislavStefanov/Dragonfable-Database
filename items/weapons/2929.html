    • Special
    Activate your weapon's special!

    Effect: 12 hits of 100% Darkness damage, amounting to 1,200% damage.
    • During Arena at the Edge of Time challenges, damage is reduced to 12 hits of 25% Darkness damage, amounting to 300% damage.
    Cooldown: 20 - [(Player LUK/50), rounded down]
    Charge Time: 10 - [(Player LUK/100), rounded down]


    Appearance


    Other information
  • Weapon is automatically upgraded to the highest level a player can use; once a new level threshold has been reached, players must relog to obtain the new version.
  • Weapons are animated; the red doom threads at the base of the scythe's blade pulsate continuously.
  • Weapons were temporary banned from Arena at the Edge of Time challenges on April 22nd, 2020; however, on April 27th, 2020, the ban was lifted and weapons' special were updated with a mechanic that lowers the weapons' special effect to 12 hits of 25% damage during the aforementioned challenges.
  • Weapons' resists were significantly updated on May 20th, 2021.
