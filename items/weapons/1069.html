Other information
  • Weapons are animated; the red doom threads pulse, the Water Orb's water runs, the Energy Orb crackles with energy, and the Darkness Orb's purple markings rise continuously.
  • Click on the weapon's orb to display and change between its available elements, or click on the grip to cycle through its available elements; this will also change its appearance.
