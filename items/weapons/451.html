    Special
    Your weapon special activates on hit.

    Effect: Applies 'Spirit of Fire', 'Spirit of Ice', or 'Spirit of Water' at random, a 40% Magic Fire/Ice/Water weapon damage DoT effect, to target for 5 turns. (Pop-up: Spirit of Independence!) (Rate: 10%)


    Spirit of Fire / Spirit of Ice / Spirit of Water Appearance


    Other information
  • Weapons are animated; a red, white, and blue orb spiral along the blade.
  • Weapons' special will cause a red, white, or blue outline to appear around the target when activated, respective to the element of the DoT effect applied.
  • Weapons' special has a 10% chance to activate, thus a special of a specific element effectively has a ~3.33% chance to apply.
  • Weapons' special may activate multiple times in a single turn, however specials will overwrite one another, and do not stack; only the most recently applied DoT will remain active.
    • All 'Spirit' DoT effects can also be overwritten by the following On-Hit DoT specials from the following weapons; this was implemented on September 8th, 2023:
      • 'DOOM': Corrupt ShadowReaper of Doom, Corrupt Skullstaff of Doom, Corrupt Twin Blades of Doom, Unreal ShadowReaper of Doom, Unreal Skullstaff of Doom, Unreal Twin Blades of Doom
      • 'Destiny': Penultimate Blinding Light of Destiny, Penultimate DragonStaff of Destiny, Penultimate Twin Blades of Destiny, Ultimate Blinding Light of Destiny, Ultimate DragonStaff of Destiny, Ultimate Twin Blades of Destiny
      • 'Spirit of Light' (and thus all 'Spirit' effects): Decus, Ferocitas, Fidelitas
