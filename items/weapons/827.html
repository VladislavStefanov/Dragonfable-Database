Special
Activate your weapon's special!

Effect: 1 hit of 200% Bacon damage; applies 'Hammerbound Crush' (-40 Crit) to target for 6 turns; attempts to shrink the target (see other information).
  • If target was already shrunk, attack instead performs 1 hit of 400% Bacon damage, with +300 Crit. (Pop-up: Cysero's hammer crushes the already shrunken foe!)
Cooldown: 5 - [(Player LUK/50), rounded down], floored at 2
Charge Time: 3 - [(Player LUK/100), rounded down], floored at 1

Other information
  • The On-Demand special will attempt to shrink the target.
    • If successful, reduces both the target's max and current HP to 90% of their previous values, as well as physically reducing the target's size. (Pop-up: Cysero's hammer uses SHRINK on <target's name>!)
    • The target can resist the shrink through their Shrink Resist (inclusive of All Resist). (Pop-up: Cysero's hammer tries... but <target's name> resist!)
    • If the target has already been shrunken, its HP and size will not be reduced again, allowing the damage from the On-Demand special to increase.
  • The On-Demand special's damage is reduced to 300% max base damage before damage modifiers (Boost, Resists, etc.) are applied during Arena at the Edge of Time challenges.
  • Skill's appearance is randomized; it will randomize between a duck, a DragonCoin, and a hammer.
