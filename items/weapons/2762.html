    Other information
  • Weapons are animated; waves in the heart-shaped window flow and shine, while water wobbles and flows toward the base of the hilt continuously.
  • Weapons' description previously specified Happy Mother's Day 2013!; this was removed alongside its 2015 re-release.
  • Weapons' names were previously prefixed with Shining, Glittering, Reflective, Luminous, Radiant, Shimmering, Sparkling, and Dazzling respectively; these were removed alongside the weapons being tiered with Roman numerals on June 9th, 2023.
  • Mothers was previously misspelled as mother's; this was corrected on June 9th, 2023.
