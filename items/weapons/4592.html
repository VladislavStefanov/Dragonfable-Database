Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Recovers 6-12% of player's max MP. (Pop-up: The power locked inside the staff helps some of your mana!) (Rate: 5%)


Appearance


Other information
  • Weapons has Mana flavor resistance.
  • Mana recovery formula, by courtesy of Ash:
    quote:

    ORIGINAL: Ash

    Mana recovered = Your Max mana * ((random(2,8) * .01) + .04)

    Random is any number from 2 to 8. This would give you back between 6-12% of your total mana on activation.
  • Weapons were initially available to purchase at Replica Weapons shop between July 17th, 2015 and July 19th, 2015.
