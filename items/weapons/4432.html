Other information
  • Weapon was initially available to purchase at Unlucky 13th Weapons shop for a price of 250 Gold, with a sellback of 25 Gold.
  • The following changes were implemented on September 26th, 2025:
    • Weapon was previously stylized as 'Unlucky 13th Sword I'.
    • Level's requirement was lowered from 20 to 13.
    • Damage range was modified from 29-53 to 37-43.
    • Weapon initially have Crit +2, Magic Def +1, Pierce Def +1, Melee Def +1, WIS +1, END +1, CHA +1, LUK -1, STR +1, Bonus +1 Stats.
    • Weapon initially have Darkness +3, Health -1 Resists.
    • Weapon initially have 25 Rarity.
    • The description's excerpt (-All resist increases damage taken, but also improves healing) was added.
