Other information
  • Weapons cannot be destroyed.
  • Weapons are animated; orb at the top of the weapon pulses with an aquamarine glow throughout all variants periodically.
  • Click anywhere on the weapon to change its appearance between staff (default) and scythes of varying magnitude; weapon will not transform if the player has insufficient Gold. (Pop-up: The Pirate's Bane deems your hoard of gold insufficient!)
