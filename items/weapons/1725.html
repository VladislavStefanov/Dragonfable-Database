Other information
  • The following changes were implemented on September 5th, 2025:
    • Weapon was renamed from 'Earth Breaker' to 'Glacier Breaker'.
    • Previously had the description: A powerful sword. The sight of it will make your enemies quake with fear.
    • Weapon was initially purchasable on Yulgar's Shop for 800 Gold, with a sellback of 80 Gold.
    • Weapon's element was changed from Metal to Ice.
    • Crit +1 stat was added.
  • Weapon's price was lowered from 500 to 250 Gold, with its sellback also lowered from 50 to 25 Gold on September 26th, 2025.
