Other information
  • Weapons are animated; the red doom threads pulse and the wheels rotate continuously; weapons also have unique animations for each available element:
    • In all appearances, the Water Orb's water runs, the Energy Orb crackles with energy, and the Darkness Orb's purple markings rise continuously.
    • In Water appearance, the water on the dragon's head runs continuously.
    • In Ice appearance, the blade sparkles periodically.
    • In Wind appearance, the blade shimmers periodically.
    • In Fire appearance, flames flicker across the dragon's head and the blade continuously, and markings on the blade pulse continuously.
    • In Stone appearance, rocks float and spin near the blade continuously.
    • In Light appearance, the wing near the dragon's head flickers with yellow flames, and the blade emits orbs of light periodically.
    • In Energy appearance, the horn on the dragon's head and the blade crackle with energy continuously, and energy runs through the blade periodically.
    • In Darkness appearance, the blade emits skulls of darkness continuously, and the black doom threads on the dragon's head wiggle continuously.
  • Click on the weapon's orbs to change between its available elements; this will also change its appearance; weapons are animated between transitions, and the selected element's orb shimmers periodically. (Pop-up: <Element>!)
