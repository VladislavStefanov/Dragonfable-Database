Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Various;
  • If equipped and slotted, weakness seeks against targets with positive Fire Resist (inclusive of All Resist) (Rate: 100%)
  • If slotted, provides +5% Crit chance with any Astravian Helm equipped (increased to +8% Crit chance with any Astravian Wings also equipped) (Rate: 100%)
  • If slotted, provides +5 Bonus with any Astravian Wings equipped (increased to +10 Bonus with any Astravian Helm also equipped) (Rate: 100%)



Other information
  • Weapons cannot be destroyed.
  • II-VI versions of this weapon have Mana flavor resistance.
  • Weapons are animated; red gem shimmers at the tip of the staff.
  • Weapons were tiered with Roman numerals and became unsellable on September 22nd, 2023.
