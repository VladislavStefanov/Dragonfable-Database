    Special
    Activate your weapon's special!

    Effect: Applies 'Strengthen' (+10 Fire Resist) to player for 5 turns inclusive; 1 hit of 200% damage; applies 'Weaken' (-10 Ice Resist) to target for 5 turns inclusive.

    Cooldown: 5 - [(Player LUK/50), rounded down], floored at 2
    Charge Time: 3 - [(Player LUK/100), rounded down], floored at 1


    Appearance


    Other information
  • Weapons' special does not stack with Frozen Claymore, Frozen Dagger, Frozen Mace, Frozen Wand, Glaive of Eternal Peace, High-Tech Turducken Carver, Hive Ranger's Halberd, or Ice Scythe's specials.
  • Weapons are animated; white shimmers appear continuously and the katana pulses with a bright white glow continuously.
  • The following changes were implemented on April 22nd, 2020:
    • Special's -Ice Resist was modified from -100 Ice Resist to target for 49 turns inclusive to -30 Ice Resist to target for 5 turns inclusive.
    • Special's damage was increased 100% damage to 200% damage.
    • Activation rate was increased from 5% to 20%.
  • Special's damage was increased from 200% to 300% on January 29th, 2021.
  • The following changes were implemented on September 8th, 2023:
    • Special now requires to land its hit to activate its -Ice effect.
    • -Ice Resist value was reduced from -30 to -10 Ice Resist.
    • Damage was reduced from 300% to 200%.
    • A +10 Fire Resist effect was added.
  • 50-80 versions' names were tiered with Roman numerals on February 8th, 2025.
