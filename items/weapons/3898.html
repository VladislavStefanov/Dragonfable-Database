Other information
  • Image shown is the default design of the axe.
  • Choose between 5 different blade designs along with the matching hilt and Core to soulforge the weapon.
  • Element is based on the Core chosen when soulforging the weapon. Possible element: Darkness, Energy, Fire, Ice, Light, Nature, Water, Wind.
  • Soulforged weapon can be merged into a new Spirit Cast.
