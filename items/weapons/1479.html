Flameguide
This staff was forged by combining Defenders Medals and Fire Gems from the cave of Glaisaurus, the Ice Dragon. You must possess a Dragon Amulet to wield it.

Location: Def. Rares
Price: N/A

Sellback: 200 Gold

Level: 8
Damage: 20-36
Element: Fire
Stats: Crit +1
Resists: Ice +1

Rarity: 7
Item Type: Staff
Damage Type: Magic
Equip Spot: Weapon
Category: Weapon

Other information
  • Weapon is animated; a red glow runs along the stem periodically.
