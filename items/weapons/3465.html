Other information
  • More information regarding the Rixty promotion can be found in the April 2010 Design Notes.
  • Weapon is automatically upgraded to the highest level a player can use; once a new level threshold has been reached, players must relog to obtain the new version.
  • Weapons are animated; the scythe's blade glistens and the central gem shines periodically.
  • Weapons are color-custom; the gems are linked to the player's trim color.
