Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Applies 'Icy Chill' (-10 Ice Resist) to target for 3 turns inclusive. (Pop-up: The power locked inside the scythe chills your foe!) (Rate: 15%)


Appearance


Other information
  • Weapons' special does not stack with Frozen Claymore, Frozen Dagger, Frozen Mace, Frozen Wand, Glaive of Eternal Peace, High-Tech Turducken Carver, Hive Ranger's Halberd, or Vanilla Ice Katana's specials.
  • Weapons initially have a discounted price of 500, 800, 1,500, 3,000, 6,000, 8,000, 10,000, and 12,000 Gold, with a sellback value of 50, 80, 150, 300, 600, 800, 1,000, and 1,200 Gold respectively.
  • The following changes were implemented on February 5th, 2021:
    • Special's duration was reduced from 5 turns to 3 turns.
    • Activation rate was increased from 5% to 10%.
  • Special's activation rate was reverted to 5% on February 13th, 2021, then increased to 7% on February 20th, 2021.
  • The following changes were implemented on September 1st, 2023:
    • Special's Resist value was lowered from -30 to -10 Ice Resist.
    • Activation rate was increased from 7% to 15%.
  • Weapons were tiered with Roman numerals on February 8th, 2025.
