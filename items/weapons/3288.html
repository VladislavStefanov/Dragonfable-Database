Special
Your weapon special activates on hit.

Effect: Recovers X MP, where X is equal to 3/4 of player's level. (Pop-up: The power of the Quadforce replenishes X MP!) (Rate: 5%)


Appearance


Other information
  • Weapons are animated; the blue cubes at the top of the staff change positions, as blue symbols glow along the stem.
  • Weapons previously had a 5% chance to recover X HP and MP on hit, where X was equal to 2.5% of player's maximum HP value; this was updated on September 28th, 2020.
  • Previously had the descriptions:
    • This staff harnesses a portion of the power of the Quadforce! Wield it to banish evil and save Lore! Or something like that. It's not that picky. Has a 5% chance on hit to recover 2.5% of your max Health and Mana. (I, II)
    • This staff harnesses a portion of the power of the Quadforce! Wield it to banish evil and save Lore! Or something like that. It's not that picky. 5% chance on hit to recover 2.5% of max HP and MP. This item requires a Dragon Amulet to use. (III)
