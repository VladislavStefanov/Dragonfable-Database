Other information
  • Weapons are animated; the blade and chain pulse, changing color continuously.
  • Click on the gem on the weapon's hilt to change between its available elements; this will also change its appearance.
  • Weapons were originally stylized as 'Shackled Fire', 'Shackled Flame', 'Shackled Coals', 'Shackled Flare', 'Shackled Pyre', and 'Shackled Blaze' respectively; they were renamed as Shackled Blaze with Roman numerals tiering on January 9th, 2026.
