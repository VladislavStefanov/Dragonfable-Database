Jova's Wrath
Khazri commissioned Cysero to build this staff in honor of her pet eagle, Jova, after it saved her life when she was attacked by a monster. Imbued with Jova's essence, the staff has a special which is triggered when you fight reptilian enemies.
(No DA Required)

Location: Cysero's Weapons
Price: 210 Dragon Coins
Sellback: 189 Dragon Coins before 24 hours, 53 Dragon Coins after 24 hours

Damage: 40-52

Stats: Crit +2, END +2, Bonus +1
Resists: Wind +2, Stone +2

Rarity: 5
Item Type: Staff
Damage Type: Magic
